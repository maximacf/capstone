About this study

Mailgine is an AI-powered email management tool developed as part of a Bachelor's capstone project at IE University. It is designed to increase the efficiency of email management for professionals who deal with high volumes of email every day.

Rather than acting fully autonomously, Mailgine keeps the user in control at every step: the AI proposes email categories, the user reviews and approves or adjusts them, and then sets their own preferences in plain language (e.g. "summarise financial emails in bullet points and draft a reply for client emails"). From there, the system automatically classifies incoming emails, generates summaries, extracts key fields such as amounts and deadlines, and drafts replies — all based on the user's own approved settings.

This questionnaire collects feedback on the tool's usability, the quality of its classifications and automated outputs, and its perceived usefulness in a professional context.
